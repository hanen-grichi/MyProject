Hello, I'm Dr. Hanen GRICHI !!!

I'm Engineering in computer science (2009) from the University ElManar Tunis Tunisia. I have my MS degree in 2011 and my Ph.D. degree in 2017 with the University of Carthage, Tunisia. Actualy I'm with the LISI-Laboratory, University of Carthage, Tunis and with School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Jinan University, Zhuhai 519070, China.

Researchs

My current research interests include reconfigurable systems, modeling, verification, validation and implementationof reconfigurable wireless sensor networks in 3D with energy harvesting.
